Introduction
MSI Center, the exclusive application created only for MSI's products, delivers optimized 
performance, and efficiency to gamers and diverse users.
Login and Register
Before using MSI Center, it is recommended to create your own account and register your 
MSI product to get your full benefits from MSI Center at once. 
To have the complete function of MSI Center, keep the Windows system login with a single 
administrator account as specific functions require higher system privileges in order to 
work (e.g. GPU Switch for Intel 13th Gen notebook models or newer, Fn/Win Key Switch, 
USB PowerShare, etc…)
Create an Account
1.  Make sure the Internet connection works properly.
2.  Activate MSI Center, move the mouse cursor to the profile icon at the upper right 
corner and click on it to enter [My Device] page
3.  Click on [Product Registration] to enter Login page.
4.  Click on [MSI Website] to link to MSI official website.

Product Registration
1.  Once a personal account is created, and logged in the account, you may start to 
register your products.
2.  The notebook you are using will be detected by MSI Center automatically, follow the 
on-screen instructions to complete the registration.
3.  To register MSI multi-media accessories which are connected to the notebook, click 
[+] and then follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration.
