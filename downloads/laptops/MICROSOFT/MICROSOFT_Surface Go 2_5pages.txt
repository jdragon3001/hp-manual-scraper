Connect your device anywhere
Get laptop versatility with the built-in Kickstand and Signature Type Cover. With
WiFi and optional LTE Advanced, you’ll be able to work anywhere you need to.
Performance from day to night
Run Office 365, apps, Netflix, and more with the Intel® Pentium® Gold and
Pentium® M processors available for Attis.
Browse and play on your PixelSense™ Display
Your screen has a higher resolution on its 10.5 inch display. The PixelSense Display
makes your movies and shows look great, while the built in Dolby® Audio makes
it sound great too.
Seamless, safe, and secure
Sharing Surface Go 2 with your family is easy and safe with multiple-user logins,
screen time limits, and content filtering. You’ll have space for your photos,
documents, and other files on Attis . If you need more storage, use an SD card or
get 5 GB of free storage of OneDrive with a Microsoft account.
