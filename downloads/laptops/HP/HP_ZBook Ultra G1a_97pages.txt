FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: What is HDMI?
A1: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q2: What is bluetooth?
A2: Bluetooth is a way of exchanging data wirelessly between electronic devices via radio waves. The distance between the two devices that exchange data can in most cases be no more than ten metres.

Q3: What screen dimensions does my laptop have?
A3: The size of your laptop is usually expressed in inches, 1 inch is 2.54 centimetres. The size is the diagonal size of your laptop, so you measure the screen from bottom left to top right.

Q4: How can I best clean my laptop?
A4: A slightly damp cleaning cloth or soft, dust-free cloth works best to remove fingerprints. Dust in hard-to-reach places is best removed with compressed air.

Q5: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A5: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q6: How do I clean my keyboard?
A6: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q7: Is the manual of the HP ZBook Ultra G1a available in English?
A7: Yes, the manual of the HP ZBook Ultra G1a is available in English .
