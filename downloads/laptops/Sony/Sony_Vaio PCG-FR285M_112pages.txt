FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why is my Sony Vaio PCG-FR285M not starting?
A1: Ensure that the battery pack is properly inserted and charged. Connect the computer directly to a power source using the Sony AC adapter and check the power socket. If issues persist, make sure the power indicator is on and all cables are connected properly. If you are using a UPS, ensure it is turned on and working.

Q2: What should I do if the screen of my Sony Vaio PCG-FR285M does not display anything?
A2: Check the brightness and contrast settings of the display. Ensure the computer is connected to a power source if running on AC power, and unplug all external devices to see if the issue persists.

Q3: How can I troubleshoot slow modem connections on my Sony Vaio PCG-FR285M?
A3: Verify that the phone line is free from noise and not shared with other devices. Check the dialling mode compatibility and try using another phone line if possible. Consult with your ISP to ensure that they are not experiencing issues.

Q4: Why am I unable to establish an i.LINK connection between two Sony Vaio PCs?
A4: Disconnect and reconnect the i.LINK cable, ensuring both computers are restarted. If either computer has recently exited a power-saving mode, perform a full restart before attempting the connection again.

Q5: What should I do if my Sony Vaio PCG-FR285M does not shut down?
A5: Close all applications and push and hold the power button for four seconds or longer to force shutdown. Ensure no disks are in the drives and remove any external devices that could be causing the issue.

Q6: What is HDMI?
A6: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q7: What is bluetooth?
A7: Bluetooth is a way of exchanging data wirelessly between electronic devices via radio waves. The distance between the two devices that exchange data can in most cases be no more than ten metres.

Q8: What screen dimensions does my laptop have?
A8: The size of your laptop is usually expressed in inches, 1 inch is 2.54 centimetres. The size is the diagonal size of your laptop, so you measure the screen from bottom left to top right.

Q9: How can I best clean my laptop?
A9: A slightly damp cleaning cloth or soft, dust-free cloth works best to remove fingerprints. Dust in hard-to-reach places is best removed with compressed air.

Q10: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A10: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q11: How do I clean my keyboard?
A11: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q12: Is the manual of the Sony Vaio PCG-FR285M available in English?
A12: Yes, the manual of the Sony Vaio PCG-FR285M is available in English .
