FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why does my computer not enter Sleep or Hibernate mode?
A1: Your computer may become unstable if the operating mode is changed before it completely enters Sleep or Hibernate mode. To restore normal operating stability: Close all open programs, restart your computer, and ensure that the computer isn't interrupted during the transition to these modes.

Q2: What should I do if my computer screen goes blank?
A2: Your computer screen may go blank if it loses power or enters a power-saving mode, such as Sleep or Hibernate. Ensure the computer is plugged into a power source and turned on. If the external display is selected for output and disconnected, use the Fn+F7 keys to switch back to the computer screen.

Q3: How can I resolve poor-quality images with the built-in MOTION EYE camera?
A3: Ensure no other application is using the camera, exit any such application, and restart the computer. Clean the lens if needed and reduce the display resolution or colors if video memory is insufficient.

Q4: Why is the battery not charging properly?
A4: If the battery power remains low after charging, the battery pack might need replacement. Disconnect from AC power, remove the battery, and inspect for any installation issues. Reinstall and try again.

Q5: What should I do if I cannot connect a USB device?
A5: Check if the USB device needs its own power supply and ensure it's connected properly. Try connecting the device to another USB port, and consult the USB device's manual for additional software requirements.

Q6: What is HDMI?
A6: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q7: What is bluetooth?
A7: Bluetooth is a way of exchanging data wirelessly between electronic devices via radio waves. The distance between the two devices that exchange data can in most cases be no more than ten metres.

Q8: What screen dimensions does my laptop have?
A8: The size of your laptop is usually expressed in inches, 1 inch is 2.54 centimetres. The size is the diagonal size of your laptop, so you measure the screen from bottom left to top right.

Q9: How can I best clean my laptop?
A9: A slightly damp cleaning cloth or soft, dust-free cloth works best to remove fingerprints. Dust in hard-to-reach places is best removed with compressed air.

Q10: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A10: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q11: How do I clean my keyboard?
A11: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q12: Is the manual of the Sony Vaio VGN-TZ1RXN available in English?
A12: Yes, the manual of the Sony Vaio VGN-TZ1RXN is available in English .
