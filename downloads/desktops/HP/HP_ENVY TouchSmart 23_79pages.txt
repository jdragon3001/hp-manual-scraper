FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: How can I troubleshoot power issues, such as the computer not turning on or shutting down unexpectedly?
A1: To troubleshoot power issues on your HP ENVY TouchSmart 23 desktop, follow these steps:
1. Shut down the computer.
2. Test the AC outlet by connecting a different electrical device to it.
3. Reconnect the computer's cables securely.
4. Try to turn on the computer:
   - If the power lights turn on, the AC adapter is working properly.
   - If the power lights remain off, the AC adapter needs replacement.
If the computer is not responding:
1. Use the Windows Task Manager to close unresponsive programs.
2. If needed, restart the computer.
If the computer shuts down unexpectedly:
- Ensure the computer is not in a hot environment.
- Check that the vents are not blocked and the internal fan is running.
For software issues:
- Restart the computer.
- Update or reinstall drivers.
- Consider refreshing or restoring the computer.

Q2: What steps should I take if the display is blank or the screen is frozen?
A2: 1. Exit Sleep mode by briefly pressing the power button or pressing a key on the keyboard like the space bar or the esc key.
2. If the screen remains blank, press the power button to turn on the computer.
3. If the images on the screen are too large, too small, or fuzzy, adjust the resolution settings by accessing Control Panel, selecting Appearance and Personalization, choosing Adjust Screen Resolution, making necessary adjustments, and clicking Apply.

Q3: How do I resolve audio issues, such as no sound or poor audio quality?
A3: To resolve audio issues such as no sound or poor audio quality on your HP ENVY TouchSmart 23 desktop, follow these steps:
1. Check that the powered speakers are connected and turned on.
2. Adjust the volume on the speakers or through the computer operating system.
3. Turn off the computer, unplug and reconnect the speakers ensuring they are properly connected.
4. Disconnect any headphones connected to the computer.
5. If the computer is in Sleep mode, exit Sleep by briefly pressing the power button.
6. Check the audio functions by accessing Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Sound and testing sound events.
7. Check the recording functions by using Sound Recorder to record and playback audio.
8. Confirm or change audio settings in Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Sound.
These steps should help you troubleshoot and resolve audio issues on your HP ENVY TouchSmart 23 desktop.

Q4: What is HDMI?
A4: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q5: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A5: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q6: How do I clean my keyboard?
A6: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q7: Is the manual of the HP ENVY TouchSmart 23 available in English?
A7: Yes, the manual of the HP ENVY TouchSmart 23 is available in English .
