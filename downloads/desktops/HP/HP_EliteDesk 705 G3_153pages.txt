FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: What is HDMI?
A1: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q2: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A2: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q3: How do I clean my keyboard?
A3: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

A4: Yes, the manual of the HP EliteDesk 705 G3 is available in English .
