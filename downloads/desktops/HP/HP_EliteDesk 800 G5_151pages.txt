FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why is my desktop not turning on?
A1: Ensure that the power cable is securely plugged into both the desktop and the power source. Press the power button on the front of the desktop firmly and hold it for a few seconds until it powers on.

Q2: How can I connect my desktop to a Wi-Fi network?
A2: Click on the Wi-Fi symbol in the bottom right corner of the desktop's screen. Select your desired Wi-Fi network from the list and enter the password when prompted. Click "Connect" to establish the connection.

Q3: How do I transfer files from a USB drive to my desktop?
A3: Insert the USB drive into one of the available USB ports on the front or back of the desktop. Open the File Explorer by clicking on the folder icon on the taskbar. Locate the USB drive under "This PC" or "Devices and Drives" and simply drag and drop the desired files from the USB drive to your preferred location on the desktop.

Q4: My desktop is running slowly, what can I do to improve its performance?
A4: Close any unnecessary applications running in the background by right-clicking on their icons in the taskbar and selecting "Close" or "Exit." You can also free up disk space by deleting unnecessary files and running a disk cleanup utility. Additionally, consider upgrading the RAM or optimizing the system through the HP Performance Advisor software.

Q5: How do I connect an additional monitor to my desktop?
A5: Locate the dedicated display ports (HDMI, DisplayPort, or VGA) on the back of the desktop. Connect one end of the appropriate cable to the desired display port on the desktop and the other end to the corresponding port on the monitor. Power on the monitor and, if necessary, press the input/source button to select the connected port.

Q6: What is HDMI?
A6: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q7: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A7: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q8: How do I clean my keyboard?
A8: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q9: Is the manual of the HP EliteDesk 800 G5 available in English?
A9: Yes, the manual of the HP EliteDesk 800 G5 is available in English .
