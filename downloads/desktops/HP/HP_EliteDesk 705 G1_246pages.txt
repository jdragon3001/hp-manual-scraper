●
The system will operate in flex modeif the total memory capacity of the DIMMs in Channel Ais not equal
to the total memory capacity of the DIMMs in Channel B. In flex mode, the channel populated with the
remainder is assigned to singlechannel. For optimalspeed, the channels should be balanced so that the
largest amount of memory is spread between the two channels. If one channel will have more memory
than the other, the larger amountshould be assigned to Channel A.For example, if you are populating
the sockets with one 2-GB DIMM, and three 1-GB DIMMs, Channel A should be populated with the 2-GB
DIMM and one 1-GB DIMM, and ChannelB should be populated with the other two 1-GB DIMMs. With this
configuration, 4-GB will run as dual channel and 1-GB will runas single channel.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why is my desktop not powering on?
A1: Check if the power cable is securely plugged into both the computer and the power outlet. Ensure the power switch on the rear of the desktop is in the "On" position. If the issue persists, it may be a power supply problem requiring professional assistance.

Q2: How can I connect additional monitors to my HP EliteDesk 705 G1?
A2: Your desktop supports multiple display outputs. Connect the additional monitors to the available video ports (such as DisplayPort or VGA) on the back of the desktop. Press the appropriate key combination (e.g. Windows key + P) to toggle between display modes such as duplicate or extend.

Q3: How do I access the BIOS settings on my desktop?
A3: Restart your computer and continuously tap the "Esc" key until the Startup Menu appears. From there, press the "F10" key to enter the BIOS setup. Please note that modifying certain settings without proper knowledge can affect system functionality, so proceed with caution.

Q4: My desktop is running slow, what can I do to improve its performance?
A4: Perform regular maintenance tasks such as clearing temporary files, uninstalling unused programs, and running a disk cleanup utility. Additionally, consider upgrading your desktop's RAM or installing a solid-state drive (SSD) for faster data access.

Q5: How can I troubleshoot sound issues on my HP EliteDesk 705 G1?
A5: First, ensure that the speakers or headphones are properly connected to the audio port. Check the volume settings in the taskbar and make sure they are not muted or set too low. If the problem persists, update the audio drivers or consult the HP support website for further troubleshooting steps specific to your model.

Q6: What is HDMI?
A6: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q7: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A7: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q8: How do I clean my keyboard?
A8: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q9: Is the manual of the HP EliteDesk 705 G1 available in English?
A9: Yes, the manual of the HP EliteDesk 705 G1 is available in English .
