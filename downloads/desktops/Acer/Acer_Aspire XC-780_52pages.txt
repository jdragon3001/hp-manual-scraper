Q2: Why is my computer not turning on?
A2: Make sure the power cable is securely plugged in and check if the power outlet is functioning properly. If the issue persists, try pressing the power button for a few seconds to reset the system.

Q3: How can I connect my computer to Wi-Fi?
A3: Navigate to the network settings by clicking on the network icon in the taskbar, then select your desired Wi-Fi network from the available options and enter the password if required. Ensure that your Wi-Fi router is turned on and within range of your computer.

Q4: What should I do if my computer is running slow?
A4: First, check for any unnecessary programs or processes running in the background and close them. Ensure that your antivirus software is up to date and perform a full system scan. You can also consider upgrading your RAM or clearing some space on your hard drive.

Q5: How can I change the screen resolution on my Acer Aspire XC-780?
A5: Right-click anywhere on the desktop and select "Display settings." From there, you can adjust the screen resolution by moving the slider to the desired level. Click on "Apply" to save the changes.

Q6: What can I do if my computer is overheating?
A6: Ensure that the computer is placed on a flat, hard surface to allow proper airflow. Clean the vents and fans regularly using compressed air to remove any dust buildup. Consider using a laptop cooling pad or an additional external fan to help with cooling.
