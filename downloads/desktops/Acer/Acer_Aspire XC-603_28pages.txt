FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why is my desktop computer not turning on?
A1: Check if the power cord of the Acer Aspire XC-603 is securely plugged into both the computer and the electrical outlet. Make sure the power button on the front of the computer is pressed firmly to turn it on.

Q2: How can I connect my Acer Aspire XC-603 to a Wi-Fi network?
A2: Access the network settings by clicking the wireless network icon in the bottom-right corner of the desktop screen. Select the desired Wi-Fi network from the available options and enter the password if prompted. Click "Connect" to establish a wireless connection.

Q3: How can I transfer files from a USB flash drive to my computer?
A3: Insert the USB flash drive into one of the USB ports on the front or back of the Acer Aspire XC-603. Open "File Explorer" from the taskbar and locate the USB drive under "This PC" or "Devices and Drives". Select the files you want to transfer, right-click, and choose "Copy". Navigate to the desired destination folder on your computer, right-click, and select "Paste" to complete the transfer.

Q4: Why is the screen resolution on my Acer Aspire XC-603 not optimal?
A4: Adjust the screen resolution by right-clicking on the desktop and selecting "Display settings". Under the "Resolution" dropdown menu, choose a higher resolution for a crisper display or a lower resolution for larger text and icons. Click "Apply" to save the changes.

Q5: How do I uninstall a software program on my desktop computer?
A5: Open the "Control Panel" by typing it in the search bar and selecting it from the results. Click on "Uninstall a program" or "Programs and Features" depending on the Windows version.
