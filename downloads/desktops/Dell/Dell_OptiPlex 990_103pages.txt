FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why is my Dell OptiPlex 990 not turning on?
A1: Ensure that the power cable is securely plugged into both the computer and the wall outlet. Check that the power supply switch at the back of the computer is in the "on" position. If the issue persists, it may be due to a faulty power supply or motherboard, and professional assistance may be required.

Q2: How can I troubleshoot overheating issues on my Dell OptiPlex 990?
A2: Ensure that the computer is placed in a well-ventilated area with sufficient airflow around it. Clean the air vents and the cooling fan regularly to remove any dust buildup that might obstruct proper airflow. Consider installing additional case fans if the internal temperature remains high.

Q3: What can I do if my Dell OptiPlex 990 is experiencing frequent system crashes or freezes?
A3: Perform a thorough scan for malware using reputable antivirus software to rule out any malicious programs causing instability. Ensure that all device drivers and the operating system are up to date. If the problem persists, a hardware component may be at fault, and it might be necessary to seek professional assistance for further diagnosis.

Q4: How can I upgrade the RAM on my Dell OptiPlex 990?
A4: Ensure that your computer is powered off and disconnected from any power source. Open the computer case by removing the screws at the back, then locate the RAM slots on the motherboard. Gently insert the new RAM modules into the slots, ensuring they are firmly seated. Close the case, reconnect all cables, and turn on the computer to verify the upgraded RAM is recognized.

Q5: What should I do if my Dell OptiPlex 990 is not recognizing my external storage device?
A5: Firstly, ensure that the external storage device is properly connected to the computer's USB port. Try connecting the device to different USB ports to check for any port-specific issues. If it still doesn't work, test the storage device on another computer to verify if it is functioning correctly. If the issue persists, there may be a problem with the USB controller or drivers, and seeking professional assistance is recommended.

Q6: Is the Dell OptiPlex 990 equipped with an Intel Core i7 processor?
A6: Yes, the Dell OptiPlex 990 is equipped with an Intel Core i7 processor. This means that it offers high performance and is suitable for demanding tasks such as multimedia editing or gaming.

Q8: What is the maximum internal memory of the Dell OptiPlex 990?
A8: The maximum internal memory of the Dell OptiPlex 990 is 16 GB. This allows for smooth multitasking and the ability to run memory-intensive applications without any performance issues.

Q9: Does the Dell OptiPlex 990 have an integrated card reader?
A9: No, the Dell OptiPlex 990 does not have an integrated card reader. However, separate card readers can be easily connected to the computer if needed.

Q10: How much total storage capacity does the Dell OptiPlex 990 have?
A10: The Dell OptiPlex 990 has a total storage capacity of 500 GB. While this may be considered low compared to newer models with larger storage capacities, it is still sufficient for most everyday use and can accommodate a decent amount of files and applications.

Q11: What is the weight of the Dell OptiPlex 990?
A11: The Dell OptiPlex 990 has a weight of 5700 g.

Q12: What is the height of the Dell OptiPlex 990?

Q13: What is HDMI?
