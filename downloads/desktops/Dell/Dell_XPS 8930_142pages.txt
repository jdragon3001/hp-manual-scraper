FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: Why is my desktop not turning on?
A1: Make sure the power cable is securely plugged into both the wall outlet and the back of the desktop. Check if the power button on the front panel is properly connected and try pressing it again.

Q2: How can I connect to Wi-Fi on my Dell XPS 8930?
A2: Click on the network icon in the system tray located at the bottom-right corner of the screen. Select your preferred Wi-Fi network from the list and enter the password when prompted. Ensure the Wi-Fi switch on the side or back of the desktop is turned on.

Q3: How can I add additional RAM to my Dell XPS 8930?
A3: Slide open the side panel of the desktop towards the rear. Locate the RAM slots on the motherboard, gently press the retaining clips on either side, and insert the RAM stick firmly into the slot. Make sure it is aligned properly before pressing it down until it clicks into place.

Q4: Why is my desktop running slowly?
A4: Close any unnecessary applications running in the background. Perform a disk cleanup to remove temporary files and free up storage space. You may also consider upgrading your RAM or optimizing the startup programs for improved performance.

Q5: How do I connect a second monitor to my Dell XPS 8930?
A5: Check the available video ports on your desktop and your monitor. Connect the appropriate cable (HDMI, DisplayPort, or VGA) from the video port on your desktop to the corresponding port on your monitor. Then, adjust the display settings in your operating system to extend or duplicate the desktop onto the second monitor.

Q6: What is HDMI?
A6: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q7: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A7: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q8: How do I clean my keyboard?
A8: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q9: Is the manual of the Dell XPS 8930 available in English?
A9: Yes, the manual of the Dell XPS 8930 is available in English .
