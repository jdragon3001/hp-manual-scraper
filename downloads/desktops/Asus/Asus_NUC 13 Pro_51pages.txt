FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q1: How can I resolve overheating issues with my ASUS NUC 13 Pro?
A1: To resolve overheating issues, ensure that the NUC is placed in a well-ventilated area. Check that all air vents are not blocked. Regularly clean the vents and fans to prevent dust buildup, which can impair cooling. It is also advisable to check the fan speed settings in the BIOS and adjust them if necessary to improve cooling efficiency.

Q2: What should I do if my ASUS NUC 13 Pro is not booting?
A2: If your ASUS NUC 13 Pro is not booting, first ensure that all external devices are disconnected. Check if the power supply is properly connected, and try using a different power outlet or power supply if available. Listen for any beep codes or error messages during startup, which may indicate specific hardware issues. You may also try resetting the BIOS settings to default using the BIOS setup.

Q3: Why isn't my ASUS NUC 13 Pro recognizing additional RAM?
A3: Ensure that the RAM modules are fully seated in the memory slots and that they are compatible with the system. The ASUS NUC 13 Pro supports DDR4 2400/2666/3200 MHz SO-DIMMs. Refer to the system specifications to ensure that the total memory does not exceed the supported limit. If problems persist, try using a single RAM module to identify if any particular module is faulty.

Q4: How can I troubleshoot display issues on my ASUS NUC 13 Pro?
A4: If you are experiencing display issues, check the HDMI and DisplayPort connections to ensure they are secure. Verify that the display settings in the BIOS are configured correctly. If you are using multiple displays, ensure that they are configured properly in the operating system settings. Updating your graphics drivers may also resolve display problems.

Q5: What should I check if my ASUS NUC 13 Pro is not connecting to Wi-Fi?
A5: Ensure that the Wi-Fi antenna is properly connected and positioned. Verify that the Wi-Fi is enabled in the BIOS settings. Check if the correct drivers for the Intel Wi-Fi card (M.2 2230) are installed and up to date. Make sure that the Wi-Fi network settings are configured correctly on your operating system.

Q6: What is HDMI?
A6: HDMI stands for High-Definition Multimedia Interface. An HDMI cable is used to transport audio and video signals between devices.

Q7: What does the download speed on the internet depend on?
A7: The download speed on the internet depends on the type of connection you have. If you have a 25 Mb/s connection this means that theoretically you should be able to download 25 MB per second.

Q8: How do I clean my keyboard?
A8: Hold the keyboard upside down and use a can of compressed air to blow away the dirt. The keys can even be cleaned with a damp cloth.

Q9: Is the manual of the Asus NUC 13 Pro available in English?
A9: Yes, the manual of the Asus NUC 13 Pro is available in English .
